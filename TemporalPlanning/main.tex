\section{Temporal Planning}

The initial project time planning is presented in detail in this section.
The project is planned to start on 18 February 2026 and conclude on 19 
June 2026, spanning 17 weeks and 2 days with an estimated total effort 
of 502 hours. The primary objective of this timeline is to meet the 
Bachelor Thesis defense deadline, scheduled for 26 June 2026, and to 
fulfill all objectives outlined in Section 3.1. However, as delays 
cannot be entirely mitigated, an extra week has been allocated as a 
buffer for potential setbacks.

According to the standards set by Polytechnic University of Catalonia, 
the bachelor's thesis carries a workload of 18 ECTS credits, equivalent 
to a sum of 540 working hours. The remaining 38 hours serve as overhead 
to accommodate potential obstacles.
